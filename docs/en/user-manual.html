Getting Started

Sign In Options

Simple Habit Tracker offers three authentication modes to fit your preferences:

Google Sign-In

Tap "Sign in with Google" to use your Google account

Email/Password

Create an account with your email and password

Device-Only Mode

Use the app without any account - perfect for privacy-focused users

Benefits of Each Mode

  • Online Accounts (Google/Email): Data syncs across devices, cloud backup, settings sync
  • Device-Only: Complete privacy, no data leaves your device, works offline

Data Migration: Device-only users can later upgrade to online accounts with seamless data transfer

Creating Your First Habit

Adding a New Habit

  1. Tap the "+" (Add) button on the main screen
  2. Enter your habit name (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water")
  3. Choose a meaningful color to identify your habit
  4. Select frequency:
    • Daily - for everyday habits
    • Weekly - specify which days
    • Custom - set your own schedule
  5. Set a target (optional) - how many times per day/week
  6. Add notes for motivation or reminders
  7. Tap "Save" to create your habit

Best Practices for Habit Creation

  • Start Small: Begin with achievable goals
  • Be Specific: Clear, measurable habits work best
  • Choose Meaningful Colors: Use colors that remind you of the habit
  • Set Realistic Frequencies: Don't overwhelm yourself

Managing Habits

Editing Existing Habits

  1. Find your habit in the list
  2. Tap the habit name or edit icon
  3. Modify any details (name, color, frequency, target, notes)
  4. Tap "Update" to save changes

Organizing Your Habits

Tracking Progress

Marking Habits Complete

Account Management

Context-Aware Account Actions

The app provides different options based on your authentication mode:

For Online Users (Google/Email):

  • Sign Out: Log out of your online account (with confirmation dialog)
  • Switch to Device-Only: Convert from online to offline mode with data backup
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and all cloud data

For Device-Only Users:

  • Sign In: Upgrade to online account with data migration options
  • Delete Data: Clear all local data from your device

Switch to Device-Only Mode (Online Users)

Online users can convert to device-only mode while preserving their data:

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: This will delete your cloud account but keep a local copy of your data
  1. Process:
    • Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Switch to Device-Only
    • Read the warning about account deletion
    • Confirm your choice
    • App automatically backs up your data locally
    • Cloud account is permanently deleted
    • You continue using the app in device-only mode
  2. What Happens:
    • All your habits and progress are preserved on your device
    • Cloud account and data are permanently deleted
    • No more data syncing across devices
    • Complete privacy - no data sent to servers
    • All dialogs and confirmations are fully localized

Delete Account (Online Users)

‚ö†Ô∏è Permanent Action - This cannot be undone!
  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Account
  2. Tap "Delete Account"
  3. Read the warning carefully (fully localized)

What gets deleted:

  • All your habit data from cloud
  • Your account permanently
  • All synced settings across devices

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Sync Problems

  • Data not syncing:
    • Check internet connection
    • Sign out and sign back in
    • Force-close and reopen the app
  • Sync conflicts:
    • App automatically resolves most conflicts
    • Manual merge may be required for major conflicts
    • Export data before major changes as backup

Authentication Issues

  • Can't sign in:
    • Verify email and password
    • Check internet connection
    • Try password reset if needed
  • Google sign-in problems:
    • Ensure Google services are available
    • Clear app cache if necessary
    • Try alternative sign-in method

Performance Issues

  • App running slowly:
    • Restart the app
    • Clear app cache
    • Check available device storage
  • Data loading problems:
    • Check internet connection for online users
    • Verify data integrity
    • Consider data re-sync

Privacy and Security

Data Privacy Commitment

Simple Habit Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Zero Data Transmission: No personal data ever leaves your device
  • No Account Required: Use all features without creating any account
  • Local Storage Only: All habit data stored securely on your device
  • No Analytics: No usage tracking or behavior analysis
  • Complete Control: You own and control all your data

Online Account Privacy:

  • Minimal Data Collection: Only collect data necessary for app functionality
  • Encrypted Storage: All cloud data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with third parties
  • Selective Sync: Choose what data to sync across devices
  • Easy Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all data anytime

Security Features

  • Secure Authentication: Industry-standard authentication protocols
  • Password Protection: Secure password storage and validation
  • Data Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted using modern standards
  • Regular Security Updates: App receives security patches automatically
  • Privacy Mode Migration: Switch from online to device-only anytime

Your Rights and Controls

  • Data Access: Export all your data in standard formats
  • Data Portability: Transfer data between devices or apps
  • Right to Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Transparency: Clear information about data usage and storage
  • Choice: Choose between privacy-focused local or convenient cloud storage

Best Practices for Users

  • Strong Passwords: Use unique, strong passwords for your account
  • Regular Backups: Export your data regularly as backup
  • Device Security: Keep your device secured with lock screen
  • Privacy Settings: Review and adjust privacy settings in the app
  • Stay Updated: Keep the app updated for latest security improvements
