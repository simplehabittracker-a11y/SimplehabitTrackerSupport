🚀 Getting Started

📱 Download & Install

  1. Visit the Google Play Store
  2. Search for "Simple Habit Tracker"
  3. Tap "Install" to download the app
  4. Open the app once installation is complete

🔐 Sign In

When you first open Simple Habit Tracker, you'll see the sign-in screen:

  1. Google Sign-In: Tap "Sign in with Google" to use your Google account
  2. Apple Sign-In: Tap "Sign in with Apple" to use your Apple ID (iOS devices)
  3. Follow the authentication prompts
  4. Your profile information will be automatically imported
💡 Tip: Using Google or Apple Sign-In ensures your data syncs across all your devices securely.

🏠 First Launch

After signing in successfully, you'll see the main dashboard featuring:

  • A personalized welcome message
  • "No habits yet" message if this is your first time
  • A floating action button (+) to create your first habit
  • Navigation tabs at the bottom (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Settings)

➕ Creating Your First Habit

📝 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Tap the floating action button (+) on the main screen
  2. Fill in the habit details:
    • Habit Name: Choose a clear, specific name (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water")
    • Description: Add optional details about your habit
    • Habit Type: Select "Daily" or "Weekly"
    • Target Count: Set how many times per day/week (default: 1)
    • Start Date: Choose when to begin tracking (default: today)
  3. Tap "Save" to create your habit

✨ Best Practices for Habit Creation

  • Be Specific: "Exercise for 30 minutes" vs. "Exercise"
  • Start Small: Begin with achievable targets you can maintain
  • One at a Time: Focus on 1-3 habits initially to avoid overwhelm
  • Set Realistic Goals: Choose targets that fit your lifestyle
  • Make it Measurable: Use concrete numbers when possible
🎯 Pro Tip: Start with habits that take less than 2 minutes to complete. Once established, you can gradually increase the complexity.

⚙️ Managing Habits

👀 Viewing Your Habits

Your habits are displayed on the main screen, showing:

  • Habit name and description
  • Current streak counter (🔥 fire icon with number)
  • Completion status for today
  • Target progress (e.g., "2/3 completed")
  • Visual completion indicator

✅ Marking Habits as Complete

  • Single Target: Tap the circle next to each habit to mark as complete
  • Multiple Targets: Tap multiple times or use the counter buttons
  • Visual Feedback: Completed habits show with a checkmark and color change
  • Undo: Tap again to undo completion if done by mistake

✏️ Editing Habits

  1. Tap on any habit to open the details view
  2. Tap the edit button (pencil icon)
  3. Modify any field except the start date (if it would invalidate existing data)
  4. Tap "Update" to save changes

🗑️ Deleting Habits

  1. Open the habit details view
  2. Tap the delete button (trash can icon)
  3. Confirm deletion in the popup dialog
⚠️ Warning: Deleting a habit cannot be undone and will remove all associated progress data.

📊 Tracking Progress

🔥 Understanding Streaks

  • Current Streak: Consecutive days/weeks of successful completion
  • Longest Streak: Your personal best record
  • Streak Breaks: Missing a day resets the current streak to zero
  • Recovery: You can rebuild streaks immediately after a break

📈 Completion Tracking

  • Daily Habits: Track every day for maximum effectiveness
  • Weekly Habits: Complete within the designated week period
  • Flexible Targets: Meet your custom target counts at your own pace
  • Completion Rate: Automatically calculated success percentage

🎯 Setting Realistic Goals

Tips for sustainable habit tracking:

  • Start with lower targets and increase gradually
  • Allow for flexibility in your schedule
  • Focus on consistency over perfection
  • Celebrate small wins and progress

👁️ Views & Analytics

📅 Daily View

  • Shows all habits scheduled for today
  • Mark completions in real-time
  • See daily progress at a glance
  • Quick access to habit actions

📊 Weekly View

  • Overview of the entire current week
  • Spot patterns in your habit completion
  • Plan ahead for upcoming days
  • Identify the most successful days

📈 Monthly View

  • Long-term progress visualization
  • Identify trends and patterns over time
  • Celebrate monthly achievements
  • Track seasonal changes in behavior

🔍 Habit Details

Each habit provides comprehensive analytics:

  • Total Completions: Lifetime count of successful completions
  • Completion Rate: Success percentage over time
  • Current Streak: Ongoing consecutive streak
  • Longest Streak: Personal record achievement
  • Progress Chart: Visual representation of your journey

⚙️ Settings

👤 Profile Section

  • View your account information
  • See profile picture and email address
  • Account details imported from Google/Apple

🔧 Preferences

  • Week Start Day: Choose Monday or Sunday as the first day of the week
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes
  • Language: Automatically detected from your device settings

🎨 Theme Settings

  • Light Mode: Clean, bright interface perfect for daytime use
  • Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes, great for evening sessions
  • Auto-Switch: Follows your device's system theme settings

💾 Data Management

📤 Export Your Data

  1. Go to Settings → Data → Export Data
  2. Choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, or PDF)
  3. Select date range or export all data
  4. Share or save the exported file to your device

☁️ Backup and Synchronization

  • Data automatically syncs with Firebase cloud storage
  • Access your habits across multiple devices
  • Automatic backup of all progress and settings
  • Real-time updates when using multiple devices
🔒 Security: All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

👤 Account Management

📤 Sign Out

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Tap "Sign Out"
  3. Confirm to log out of your account

🗑️ Delete Account

⚠️ Permanent Action: This action cannot be undone!
  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Tap "Delete Account"
  3. Read the warning message carefully
  4. Confirm deletion if you're absolutely sure

What gets permanently deleted:

  • All your habit data and progress
  • Your account and profile information
  • All associated settings and preferences
  • Access to the app with this account

🔧 Troubleshooting

❓ Common Issues

🔄 Habits not syncing between devices?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Sign out and back in to refresh the connection
  • Ensure you're using the same account on all devices
  • Contact support if issues persist

💥 App crashes or freezes?

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the Play Store
  • Clear the app cache in device settings

🔐 Can't sign in to your account?

  • Verify your Google/Apple account is working in other apps
  • Check if account services are enabled in device settings
  • Try clearing the app's cached data
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection

📊 Missing data or progress?

  • Ensure you're signed into the correct account
  • Check if data syncing is enabled in settings
  • Wait a few minutes for cloud sync to complete
  • Contact support for data recovery assistance
