Welcome to Simple Habit Tracker

A powerful habit tracking app designed to help you build and maintain daily and weekly habits with comprehensive streak tracking, progress analytics, and goal setting capabilities.

🔒 Privacy & Security

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Download the app from Google Play Store
  2. Sign in with Google or Apple account
  3. Create your first habit using the + button
  4. Track daily progress by marking habits complete
  5. Celebrate your growing streaks and achievements!
